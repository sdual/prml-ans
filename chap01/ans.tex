\section{Answers to exercises chapter 1}
1.2 \\
The error function is given by
\begin{eqnarray}
 \tilde{E} = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{n=1}^N
  \left \{
   y(x_n, \bf{w}) - t_n
  \right \}^2 + \frac{1}{2} \| \bf{w} \|^2 .
\end{eqnarray}
